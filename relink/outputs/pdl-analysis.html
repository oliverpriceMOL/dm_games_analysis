Vertical Inference & Theme Transparency

Which PDL features promote or inhibit vertical inference? Acceleration = ratio of last to first inter-correct interval (<1 means players sped up). Transparency = % of players who got their 3rd row first-try after solving 2.

Summary

Acceleration by PDL Feature

Mean acceleration factor per category. Lower = players sped up more (stronger vertical inference). The dashed line marks 1.0 (no change).

Transparency by PDL Feature

Mean theme transparency (% first-try on 3rd row after 2 correct) per category. Higher = theme is more deducible.
